PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: 2026

MakeMHz LLC ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use,
and protect information in connection with our software, firmware, and hardware products ("Software" and "Product").

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the Software and in connection with the operation of the
Product, including software updates and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited technical information necessary to operate, secure, and maintain the Software and Product. This may include:

- Device identifiers (such as unique hardware or firmware IDs)
- Firmware version and update status
- Software integrity and authenticity data

We do not intentionally collect personal information such as names, addresses, or contact details through the Software.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- Providing software updates and related services
- Verifying device authenticity and Software integrity

- Detecting, preventing, and responding to security issues or abuse

Information collected by the Software may be used to determine eligibility for software updates, online services, and
other functionality.

MakeMHz LLC may deny, restrict, or revoke access to updates or services if the Company reasonably determines that a device
or Software instance is unauthorized, modified, tampered with, or otherwise in violation of applicable license terms.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your information.

We may share limited technical data with service providers that assist in operating our infrastructure (such as update
delivery systems or hosting providers), solely for the purposes described in this Policy.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Policy, including
maintaining service functionality, enforcing license terms, and ensuring system security.

6. Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect the information we collect from
